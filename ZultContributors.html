The Rise of Zult

It all started when a message appeared in the Discord chat from an unfamiliar face and name. He went by the name “Possibly Spam.” No one had ever seen or heard from him before this. His first message was, “So happy to be here.” He then started to praise Layer Zero, saying, “Gud Tech.” Everyone thought well of him, and the Layer Zero team and community accepted him with open arms. However, this acceptance would soon lead to historical chaos.

Possibly Spam then initiated a poll, making the community choose a name for themselves between “LAZERS” and “ZEROS.” Everyone was happy to have the right to choose, but no one knew this was part of his evil plan to create chaos and enforce his dictatorship.

After the votes were tallied, he did something unexpected. He ignored the community's input and chose the name “ZULT” without consulting anyone. The community was shocked and asked for an explanation, but Possibly Spam proceeded to ban and warn everyone who questioned him.
